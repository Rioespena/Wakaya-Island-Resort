ACTIVITIES

Experience the Ultimate Island Adventure at Wakaya

Dive into vibrant coral reefs with snorkeling and scuba diving, or explore the serene waters with kayaking and paddleboarding. Hike through lush landscapes and take in breathtaking views, then unwind with traditional spa treatments using local ingredients. Immerse yourself in Wakaya's culture with village visits and cooking classes, creating unforgettable memories in this island paradise.

DIVING

Explore vibrant underwater worlds with thrilling diving experiences, where you can discover colorful coral reefs, diverse marine life, and crystal-clear waters. Perfect for both beginners and seasoned divers seeking an unforgettable adventure.

FISHING

Experience the thrill of fishing in pristine waters, where you can cast your line and reel in a variety of fish while surrounded by stunning natural beauty. Whether you're a novice or an expert, it's a peaceful and rewarding activity for all.

SAILING

Set sail on tranquil waters and enjoy the freedom of the open sea. Whether you're a seasoned sailor or a beginner, experience the thrill of gliding through crystal-clear waters while taking in breathtaking coastal views. Perfect for those seeking adventure or relaxation on the water.

TENNIS

Enjoy a friendly match under the stars on our night-lit tennis court, a fun experience for tennis-lovers or those looking for a unique way to spend time.

YOGA AND MEDITATION

Take a deep breath and immerse yourself in the tranquility of our island paradise with self-guided yoga and meditation sessions, whether in your bure or tucked into your favorite secluded spot on the island. Yoga equipment available in the Fitness Bure.

CULTURAL EXPERIENCES

Experience true Fijian traditions including a Kava Welcome Ceremony, dances, craft weaving, carving, rituals, and more. At Wakaya, we pride ourselves to guard and encourage Fijian traditions and to learn the beauty of Fiji’s inhabitants, who promote their culture with overwhelming joy and love.

GOLF

Hit the links at Wakaya’s small but challenging 9-hole golf course that winds through an old coconut plantation and lush tropical forests.

GYM

Grab some mallets from our Sports Bure and enjoy some friendly competition with a game on our beautiful croquet court, adjacent to our tennis court.

HIKING

From leisurely trails up to Chieftains Leap to more challenging rocky hikes from the hill to the sea, enjoy your own self-led hiking adventure amidst the beautiful nature of Wakaya Island.

RECREATION ROOM

Challenge your skills with a game of pool in our Recreation Room, perfect for rainy day entertainment or a shaded break from the sun.

SNORKELING

Experience the beauty and tranquility of our crystal blue waters, home to vibrant coral reefs and an array of tropical fish.
