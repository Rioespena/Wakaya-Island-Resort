Wakaya Island, a 2200-acre land, is blessed with nutrient-rich volcanic soil in the Fiji archipelago. Farming is a way of living in Fiji and is no exception to the Wakaya community.

We create authentic menus using what is grown on the island, as we believe that anything naturally grown is essential for a long and healthy life.
