Experience Your Perfect Isalnd Hideaway

Wakaya Island Resort offers luxurious accommodations designed for ultimate relaxation and privacy. Choose from charming beachfront bures or expansive villas, each with stunning ocean views, private plunge pools, and open living spaces. Blending traditional Fijian style with modern comforts, our rooms provide the perfect retreat in an idyllic tropical paradise. Find your perfect hideaway and experience the serene beauty of Wakaya Island.

BURES

ONE & TWO BEDROOM SUITES

Our ten free-standing waterfront Fijian cottage suites, or bures, feature cathedral ceilings, woven bamboo wall coverings, and indigenous yaka timber flooring. Oh, and we can't forget the air conditioning, high-speed WiFi, and luxurious bathroom with oversized soaking tub, of course!
