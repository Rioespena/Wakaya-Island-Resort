Where Time Slows and Luxury Flows

Escape to Wakaya Island Resort, a private sanctuary of unrivaled beauty in the heart of Fiji. Surrounded by turquoise waters, lush tropical landscapes, and pristine white sand beaches, Wakaya offers an exclusive retreat for those seeking luxury, serenity, and adventure.

Whether indulging in world-class cuisine, rejuvenating at the spa, or exploring the vibrant marine life, every moment on Wakaya is an invitation to experience paradise like never before.

SPA

The Wakaya Club & Spa is a luxury private island resort located in Fiji's Lomaiviti Group, offering an exclusive retreat amidst pristine natural beauty. The resort features ten free-standing waterfront Fijian cottage suites, known as bures, each providing an intimate and luxurious experience. Guests can indulge in a variety of activities, including scuba diving, snorkeling, hiking, and water sports, all set against the backdrop of the island's lush landscapes and vibrant coral reefs. The on-site Breeze Spa offers rejuvenating treatments using indigenous ingredients, providing a sanctuary for relaxation and well-being.

ACTIVITIES

Wakaya offers a variety of activities that showcase its natural beauty and cultural richness. Visitors can enjoy snorkeling and scuba diving to explore vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life. For those looking to stay active, kayaking and paddleboarding provide a peaceful way to navigate the tranquil waters. Nature walks and hiking trails allow guests to immerse themselves in the lush landscapes, offering stunning views of the surrounding environment. The island also offers rejuvenating spa treatments, featuring traditional therapies using locally sourced ingredients. To experience the local culture, visitors can participate in village visits and cooking classes, giving them a deeper understanding of the island's heritage.
