SPA

Spa in Wakaya

Rejuvenate your mind and body with tranquil spa treatments inspired by nature. Indulge in soothing massages with locally sourced oils, detoxifying body wraps, and revitalizing facials infused with tropical botanicals. Let the calming ocean breeze enhance your yoga and meditation sessions, or soak in the serenity of a private outdoor bath surrounded by lush greenery. Experience pure relaxation in the heart of paradise.

Massage Services

East Meets West Massage

80 minutes

Experience optimal well-being for the mind, body, and spirit. Our therapists combine the holistic healing techniques of the East, restoring the body's natural balance, with the more specific muscle manipulation of the West. We offer this treatment both in the spa or the privacy of your own bure.

Hot Stone Massage

80 minutes

Release tension, restore balance, and increase positive energy flow with a hot stone massage. Warm stones are strategically placed on your body and combined with our own cold-pressed virgin coconut massage oil to relax tense muscles, increasing circulation in the body.

Foot Massage

30 minutes

Receive many of the benefits of a tropical body massage without spoiling your hair (or makeup). You'll start with a relaxing foot-bathing ritual, then our expert therapist will massage your calves and work on the pressure points of the feet to release tension and restore your vitality.

Skincare Services

Dilo Rescue Wrap

50 minutes

Soothe, moisturize, and heal sunburn, dehydration, or even insect bites with our healing blend of Dilo and virgin coconut oils, aloe vera, and passionflower extract, followed by a nourishing butter massage.

Paradise Sugar Glow

50 minutes

Get glowing skin with a coconut milk and exotic dilo oil massage, followed by a pure sugar cane exfoliation.

Tropical Renewal Facial

60 minutes

Improve skin tone and increase hydration with this phenomenal facial care treatment. This facial uses an ancient blend of Dilo and Papaya enzymes from the tropical isles of Fiji, and is great for both men and women.

Mani & Pedi Services

Tropical Paradise Manicure

With polish: 60 minutes
Without polish: 50 minutes

Indulge in a rejuvenating hand treatment: a coconut-milk bath, followed by a sugar rub exfoliation with essential oils and coconut oil. A warm body butter wrap moisturizes your skin and finally your nails are polished to perfection with your choice of polish.

Tropical Paradise Pedicure

With polish: 60 minutes
Without polish: 50 minutes

Indulge in a rejuvenating foot treatment: a coconut-milk bath, followed by a sugar rub exfoliation with essential oils and coconut oil. A warm body butter wrap moisturizes your feet and finally your toenails are polished to perfection with your choice of polish.
